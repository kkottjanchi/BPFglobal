Pungmul Challenge

SNS Experience Zone

Spread the charm of K-Pungmul to the world through SNS

Challenge Overview

A global SNS event where you can receive special goods by uploading Pungmul experience videos to SNS and using designated hashtags. Share the beauty of K-Pungmul with people all over the world!

Challenge Features

  • Film and upload Pungmul experience videos
  • Participate in multilingual hashtag events
  • Receive goods immediately upon participation confirmation
  • Operates continuously during festival period
  • Support for various SNS platforms worldwide
Free Participation Instant Goods Always Open Global Event

How to Participate

1

Experience Pungmul

Experience Pungmul instruments at the Global Zone Pungmul Challenge booth and film a video

2

Upload Video

Upload your Pungmul experience video to your SNS account

3

Add Hashtags

Complete your post by including the designated hashtags

4

Receive Goods

Show your upload screen to the staff and receive special goods!

Experience Zone Guide

Pungmul Challenge Zone

📍 Location

Global Zone Pungmul Challenge Booth

Street B Zone near Bupyeong Station

🕐 Operating Hours

September 27 (Sat) ~ 28 (Sun), 2025

11:00 AM ~ 8:00 PM Always Open

🎯 Experience Content

  • Pungmul instrument experience
  • Photo zone shooting with life-size cutouts
  • Mat rest area provided
  • Watch reference videos and follow along

Participation Benefits & Guidelines

📋 Participation Requirements

  • Have SNS account (public setting)
  • Include at least 2 hashtags
  • Show upload screen to staff
  • Once per person (multiple participation possible)

⚠️ Important Notes

• Videos may be used for festival promotion purposes
• Face mosaic processing available for privacy protection
• Copyright-free background music recommended
