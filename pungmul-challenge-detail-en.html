Pungmul Challenge

SNS Experience Zone

Spread the charm of K-Pungmul to the world through SNS

Challenge Overview

A global SNS event where you can receive special goods by uploading Pungmul experience videos to SNS and using designated hashtags. Share the beauty of K-Pungmul with people all over the world!

Challenge Features

  • Film and upload Pungmul experience videos
  • Participate in multilingual hashtag events
  • Receive goods immediately upon participation confirmation
  • Operates continuously during festival period
  • Support for various SNS platforms worldwide
Free Participation Instant Goods Always Open Global Event

How to Participate

1

Experience Pungmul

Experience Pungmul instruments at the Global Zone Pungmul Challenge booth and film a video

2

Upload Video

Upload your Pungmul experience video to your SNS account

3

Add Hashtags

Complete your post by including the designated hashtags

4

Receive Goods

Show your upload screen to the staff and receive special goods!
