Pungmul Class

Traditional Korean pungmul Experience

A special time to learn and experience traditional Korean pungmulnori directly

Program Overview

A special program where foreign tourists can directly experience the traditional Korean Pungmulnori instruments.

Program Features

  • Traditional instrument lessons (30 minutes)
  • English rhythm sheet music provided
  • Korean/English completion certificate
  • Commemorative photos at photo zone
  • Translation support & guide
Free Participation Translation Support Certificate Provided Souvenirs Included
