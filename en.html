Festival Overview

Korea's Representative Traditional Culture Festival

Korea's representative traditional culture festival with 29 years of history and tradition, attracting over 600,000 visitors annually as Incheon's flagship festival.

MCST Designated Cultural Tourism Festival

Selected as a Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism Cultural Tourism Festival for 2024~2025, chosen from only 25 out of over 1,200 festivals nationwide for two consecutive years.

Modern Heritage of Pungmul

A participatory festival that reinterprets traditional pungmul in a modern way, allowing people of all ages and nationalities to enjoy together.
