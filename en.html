Festival Overview

Korea's Representative Traditional Culture Festival

29 Years of History and Tradition

Started in 1997, the Bupyeong Pungmul Festival is Korea's representative traditional culture festival, attracting over 600,000 visitors annually as Incheon's signature festival.

Designated Cultural Tourism Festival

Selected as one of only 25 festivals out of 1,200 nationwide by the Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism for 2024-2025.

Modern Interpretation of Pungmul

A participatory festival that reinterprets traditional pungmul performances in a modern way, enjoyable for all ages and nationalities.
