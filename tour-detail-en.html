Bupyeong Walking Tour

Walking Through Bupyeong

Discover the new charm of Bupyeong through shopping tours in the original downtown commercial districts

Program Overview

The Walking Bupyeong is a local stamp tour program where tourists explore Bupyeong Market, Bupyeong Cultural Street, and Bupyeong Underground Shopping Center with a commentator and participate directly in mission programs.

Tour Highlights

  • Bupyeong Shopping District Tour (3 and a half hour course)
  • Professional local guide and safety personnel accompaniment
  • English interpretation support
  • Meals provided
  • Mission programs and vouchers
Free Participation Translation Support Meals Provided Guide Accompaniment

Tour Course Guide

1

Bupyeong 4-dong Catholic Church Main Gate

Attendance check and tour start at gathering place

Gathering time: 11:00 / 15:30

2

Bupyeong Kkang Market Entrance + Kkang Market Customer Rest Area

Introduction to the history and culture of Bupyeong Market

Bupyeong Market mission tour (fruit, side dishes, photo printing vouchers provided)

Bibimbap making experience and meal

3

Bupyeong Cultural Street Section + Photo Zone

Introduction to Cultural Street and group photo session

(If crowded, commentary may be conducted at the Bupyeong Underground Shopping Center)

4

Bupyeong Underground Shopping + Underground Shopping Experience Space

Underground shopping commentary and experience programs

Satisfaction survey and photo zone photography

Gift delivery upon mission completion

5

Tour End

Move to Bupyeong Pungmul Festival venue and festival viewing
