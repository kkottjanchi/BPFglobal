Walking Through Bupyeong

Bupyeong Underground Tour

Discover the new charm of Bupyeong through the underground shopping center

Program Overview

A special tour program to experience the history and culture of Bupyeong by walking through Bupyeong Underground Shopping Center, a hidden gem of Bupyeong, with a local guide.

Tour Highlights

  • Bupyeong Underground Cultural Tour (3-hour course)
  • Professional local guide accompaniment
  • Multilingual interpretation support
  • Local business discount coupons provided
  • Stamp tour leaflet provided

Tour Guidelines

Pre-participation Information

Attire: Comfortable shoes and clothing (3-hour walking)

What to bring: Personal water bottle, camera (optional)

Language: Korean, English, other languages with Translation Support

Accessibility: Stairs required due to underground shopping center characteristics

Tour Tips

Photo zones: Multiple photo zones prepared for SNS verification shots

Shopping: Free shopping time and discount benefits after tour

Restaurants: Guide-recommended local restaurant information provided

Cultural experience: Listen to stories about Bupyeong's history and culture
