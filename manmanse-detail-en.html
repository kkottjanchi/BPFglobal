Manmanse United

Manmanse United

Special night parade with multinational participants

Program Overview

A multinational pungmul parade that crowns the festival's grand finale. A special time when foreign participants march through Bupyeong's night streets wearing hanbok and carrying the Korean flag, spreading Korea's beautiful traditional culture to the world.

Parade Highlights

  • Pungmul team with multinational participants
  • Taegeukgi and international flags performance
  • Traditional hanbok wearing experience
  • SNS verification and group photo session
  • Special memories on the last night of the festival
