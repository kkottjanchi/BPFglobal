Manmanse United

Manmanse United

A special night parade with multinational participants

Program Overview

A multinational pungmul parade that crowns the festival's grand finale. A special time when foreign participants march through Bupyeong's night streets wearing hanbok and carrying the Korean flag, spreading Korea's beautiful traditional culture to the world.

Parade Highlights

  • Pungmul team with multinational participants
  • Taegeukgi and international flags performance
  • Traditional hanbok wearing experience
  • SNS verification and group photo session
  • Special memories on the last night of the festival
Free Participation Hanbok Provided Souvenirs Night Parade

Parade Timeline

19:00

Participant Assembly & Check-in

Participant registration confirmation and reception at Global Zone booth

Distribution of participation certificates and program guidance

19:10

Hanbok Fitting & Costume Preparation

Selection and fitting of hanbok according to individual sizes

Professional staff assistance with hanbok fitting and adjustments

19:30

Pungmul Training & Practice

Basic pungmul rhythm and parade movement training

Instruction on kkwaenggwari, jing, buk instruments and chanting practice

19:45

Parade Route & Formation Practice

Practice of actual parade route and formation arrangement

Safety guidelines and emergency situation response training

20:00

🎉 Manmanse Parade Begins

Pungmul parade march through Bupyeong Cultural Street area

March carrying Taegeukgi and international flags with "Manmanse" chants

20:30

Group Performance & Closing Ceremony

Joint pungmul performance by all participants on the main stage

Climax with "Manmanse" chants in various languages

20:45

Commemorative Photography & Conclusion

Group photo session and sharing impressions with all participants

Individual/group photo session followed by hanbok return and souvenir distribution

Important Information

Participation Guide & Benefits

Eligibility & Requirements

👥 Priority Participants

  • Foreign tourists (advance registration required)
  • International students
  • Multicultural families
  • Koreans accompanying foreigners

📋 Participation Requirements

  • Available for continuous participation from 7:00 PM to 8:50 PM (1 hour 50 minutes)
  • Physical ability to walk and participate standing for about 30-40 minutes
  • Interest in Korean traditional culture and enthusiastic participation attitude
  • Consent to photography/videography and SNS usage

Benefits Provided

🎁 Free Items Provided

  • Traditional hanbok rental (men's and women's sizes, must be returned on the day)
  • Hanbok sizes: XS~4XL available, professional staff size measurement and fitting assistance
  • Taegeukgi and cheering props
  • Pungmul instruments (simple accessories)
  • Participation souvenirs and certificate

📸 Special Services

  • Professional photographer accompaniment and photography
  • Group and individual photo sessions
  • Participation in SNS hashtag events
  • Photos provided at a later date
