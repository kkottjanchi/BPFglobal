Creative tips

Design: Get a taste of what QR Codes can do with your artwork

Color: Leverage the natural harmony of traditional materials

Batch: Arrange them in order from smallest to largest

Completeness: Don't be greedy and keep it simple and clean.

Experience etiquette

Share: The Table tool is shared by 6 people

Organize: Clean up after yourself

Time: Be respectful of other participants' time

Help: Feel free to contact your representative with any questions
