K-Content Playground

Enjoyable cultural experience through Korean traditional games

Program Overview

A special play space where you can experience Korean traditional games from childhood memories with foreigners and enjoy together beyond language and cultural barriers.

Playground Features

  • Main games (team play with game leaders)
  • Sub games (individual traditional games)
  • Dalgona prizes for winners
  • Regular operation every hour (30 minutes each)
  • Multilingual game rules guide
