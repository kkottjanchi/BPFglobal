K-Content Playground

K-Content Playground

Enjoyable cultural experience through Korean traditional games

Program Overview

A special play space where you can experience Korean traditional games from childhood memories with foreigners and enjoy together beyond language and cultural barriers.

Playground Features

  • Main games (team play with game leaders)
  • Sub games (individual traditional games)
  • Dalgona prizes for winners
  • Regular operation every hour (30 minutes each)
  • Multilingual game rules guide
무료 참여 Dalgona Gift Team Play Always Open

Available Traditional Games

Main Games (Led by Game Leaders)

🌸

Mugunghwa Flower Has Bloomed

Korea's representative traditional game, most popular among foreigners

🦘

Rubber Band Jump

Traditional game that develops rhythm and teamwork

🏃

Other team play

Play a variety of traditional games depending on your group size

Serve Play (Personal Experience)

🦶

Jegi-chagi

A Korean folk game in which players balance themselves on one foot and use one hand to kick a paper or cloth-wrapped jegi over and over again without letting it fall to the ground.

🌪️

paengi-chigi

Spinning with a traditional spinning wheel

✋

gonggi-nori

A dexterity game that involves throwing small stones (balls) up in the air, catching them on the ground, and then catching them as they come back down.

🎲

Yutnori

Traditional Korean board games
